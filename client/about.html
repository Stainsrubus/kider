About Us

Vision

" At Mother’s Kidbee Pre School, we envision a nurturing and stimulating environment where every child blossoms with confidence, creativity, and curiosity. Our goal is to lay the foundation for lifelong learning by fostering individual growth, cultural values, and a love for exploration through innovative teaching and personalized care. Together, we aim to build a brighter future, one tiny step at a time. "

Mission

" Our mission is to provide a loving, secure, and nurturing environment where care is at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to fostering each child’s emotional, social, and intellectual growth by offering individualized attention and compassionate guidance. Through creative learning experiences and value-based education, we aim to cultivate confident, kind, and happy learners, ensuring every child feels cherished and supported every step of the way. "

R. Jeganathan

Founder

Founder’s Message

" Mother’s Kidbee Pre School, we are committed to creating a nurturing environment where children are cared for with love and attention while being guided toward holistic development. Our goal is to ensure that every child not only learns but also grows with confidence, creativity, and joy.

As the founder and CEO, I take pride in building a space where individualized care is at the core of our philosophy. We emphasize the development of fine motor skills, gross motor skills, cognitive abilities, and social-emotional growth for each child. By focusing on every child’s unique potential, we foster a supportive atmosphere that inspires exploration, creativity, and self-confidence.

With over 11 years of experience in early childhood education, our journey continues with the same passion and dedication. Together with our dedicated team, we strive to create a solid foundation for each child future.

Thank you for entrusting us with your child’s early years. Let’s work together to nurture and guide them toward a brighter tomorrow. "

R. Sheeba

Principal

Principal’s Message

" Welcome to Mother’s Kidbee Pre School, where nurturing young minds and hearts has been our passion for over 11 years. At our school, we believe that every child is unique and deserves a safe, caring, and joyful environment to learn, grow, and thrive.

Individual care is at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to focusing on the holistic development of each and every child, emphasizing fine motor skills, gross motor skills, cognitive abilities, and social-emotional growth. Our approach ensures that every child is guided with love and attention, helping them reach their fullest potential at their own pace.

With over a decade of experience, our dedicated team works tirelessly to create a strong foundation for lifelong learning, instilling confidence, values, and curiosity in every little one who walks through our doors.

Thank you for trusting us with your child’s early years. Together, let’s create a bright and promising future for them. "

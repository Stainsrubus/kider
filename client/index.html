Age-Based Programs

Our age-specific programs nurture young minds through tailored activities that inspire learning and creativity. Each stage focuses on building essential skills in a safe, fun, and engaging environment.

Pre-Play Group

(1.5 Years)

Personalized care in a nurturing environment that fosters early growth and development in a fun, secure setting.

Playgroup

(2 Years)

A gathering of children of similar ages, providing a space for them to develop social skills at their own pace.

PKG

(3 Years)

Where children develop independence, social skills, and basic concepts through fun activities.

Junior Kindergarten

(4 Years)

Helps children build communication, problem-solving, and social skills through engaging activities.

Senior Kindergarten

(5 Years)

Strengthens literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills for a confident transition to primary school.

Weekend Classes

Explore Our Weekend Classes

At Mother’s Kidbee Pre School, we offer a wide variety of weekend activities designed to nurture your child’s creativity, intelligence, and physical skills.

Yoga

Promotes physical fitness, mental relaxation, and mindfulness for holistic well-being.

Chess

Sharpens critical thinking, improves concentration, and nurtures strategic decision-making skills in a fun way.

Skating

Builds balance, coordination, and confidence while making fitness fun and engaging.

Abacus

Enhances mental math skills, concentration, and cognitive development through engaging hands-on learning.

Silambam

Enhances physical fitness, agility, and discipline while preserving a rich cultural heritage.

Vedic Math

Simplifies complex calculations, boosts mental agility, and fosters a love for mathematics through ancient techniques.

Teakwondo

Builds discipline, self-confidence, and physical fitness, empowering students with self-defense skills and a strong mindset.

Handwriting

Enhances fine motor skills, boosts creativity, and fosters a sense of discipline and self-expression in students.

Phonetic Reading

Builds strong language foundations, improves pronunciation, and boosts confidence in communication skills.

Montessori Teacher Training Programs

We offer comprehensive Montessori teacher training programs designed for adults who are passionate about early childhood education. Our programs equip educators with the knowledge and skills to create a nurturing, child-centered learning environment. Through practical training and theoretical understanding, we prepare teachers to implement Montessori principles effectively in the classroom, fostering independent, confident, and curious learners.

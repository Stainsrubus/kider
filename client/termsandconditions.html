Terms and Conditions

Admission Policy

  • Admission to Mother's kidbee Playschool is subject to availability and at the discretion of the management.
  • All necessary forms and documents must be completed and submitted before the child's enrollment.

Fees and Payment

  • Fees must be paid in advance as per the agreed schedule.
  • A late payment fee may be applied for delayed payments beyond the due date.
  • Fees are non-refundable except in cases outlined in the refund policy.

Attendance and Timings

  • Regular attendance is encouraged to ensure the child's consistent development.
  • Parents/guardians must adhere to the drop-off and pick-up timings to avoid inconvenience.

Health and Safety

  • Parents must inform the school of any medical conditions, allergies, or special needs of the child.
  • Children showing signs of illness may be required to stay home until fully recovered to ensure the safety of others.
  • Mother's kidbee Playschool maintains high hygiene standards and follows safety protocols to ensure a secure environment for the children.

Behavior and Discipline

  • Mother's kidbee Playschool promotes a positive environment and encourages good behavior among children.
  • Any instances of disruptive behavior will be addressed appropriately in consultation with the parents.

Activities and Programs

  • The playschool curriculum includes a mix of educational and recreational activities.
  • Mother's Kidbee Playschool reserves the right to modify the activities or schedule as needed.

Personal Belongings

  • Parents are responsible for labeling their child's belongings.
  • The school is not liable for lost or damaged personal items.

Photography and Media Consent

  • Mother's kidbee Playschool may use photos or videos of children for internal purposes or promotional materials.
  • Parents/guardians must notify the school in writing if they do not consent to media usage.

Emergency Situations

  • In case of an emergency, parents will be contacted immediately using the contact information provided.
  • Parents/guardians authorize Mother's kidbee Playschool to seek medical attention for their child if required.

Termination of Enrollment

  • Mother's kidbee Playschool reserves the right to terminate a child's enrollment if policies are not adhered to or if the child's presence poses a safety concern.

Amendments to Terms and Conditions

  • Mother's kidbee Playschool reserves the right to amend these terms and conditions. Parents/guardians will be informed of any changes in advance.
